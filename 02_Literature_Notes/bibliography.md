## How to bulk-download PDFs locally

Run `02_Literature_Notes/download_papers.sh` from your terminal (on your own machine, not in this Cowork sandbox — the sandbox blocks direct PDF downloads). The script will populate `02_Literature_Notes/pdfs/` with the arXiv PDFs.

D-group classical references (Dawid-Skene, Bradley-Terry) require Purdue library or JSTOR institutional access for the canonical versions. Free near-copies are linked in-line above where available.

Story 01

The headline certainty shrinks under realistic noise

Standard leaderboard significance treats all votes as IID, but your hierarchical model reclassifies a major share of frontier gaps as statistically indistinguishable.

Story 02

Most uncertainty is structural, not sampling noise

Variance is dominated by interaction effects, so collecting more battles alone will not solve confidence inflation in close matchups.

Story 03

Fragility concentrates among top-tier model comparisons

Flipped pairs cluster around strongest models, which is where benchmark-based procurement and deployment choices are most sensitive.
